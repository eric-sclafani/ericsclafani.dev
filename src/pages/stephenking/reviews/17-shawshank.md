Most people don't know SK wrote this as they're more familiar with the movie. And rightly so! I usually don't think SK movie adaptations are good (looking at you Pet Semetary 2019 😠), but I think this adaption is _better_ than the novella. A rare opinion from me.

The director and screenwriter, **Frank Darabont** (who also did the movie adaptation of _The Green Mile_), took the novella and added more to its characters. That was one of my complaints about SK's novella; not enough time spent fleshing out the side characters. But thankfully, the movie rectifies this.

So far, really enjoying this book. I like how even though technically the boys are competing with eachother, there's still a level of camaraderie between some of them.

One really interesting thing is in the book, they have to walk at least 4mph. That's _insanely fast_ for a walk. After some research, I discovered that this was a mistake in the book. The movie adaptation does rectify this by making it 3mph, a much easier pace to keep long term.
